About Dan Santos

I was born on July 15, 1974 in St. Vincent's Hospital in Greenwich Village, NYC. I was raised in the Chelsea section of Manhattan, first living on 15th Street between 8th and 9th Avenues, then moving up to 28th Street between 8th and 9th Avenues.

I attended St. Bernard's Elementery School in the Village, before moving on to Xavier High School on 16th Street, from where I graduated in 1992. I then decided to attend Syracuse University, where I majored in Advertising Design in Syracuse's College of Visual and Performing Arts, before graduating in 1996.

After graduating I decided to stay upstae and worked as a graphic designer before deciding to move back home and work for a global B2B advertising agency named Stein IAS, where I am now Director of Creative Services.

Our clients include:

  • Wester Union Business Solutions
  • 1010 Data
  • Merck Animal Health
  • Many more boring B2B clients ;)

My most proud moment is when my son, Nicolas, was born on March 7, 2007 at 12:28pm, measuring 20.5" and weighing 8lbs, 7 oz. Every year we go to Disney World for his birthday, one of mine and his favorite vacation spots.

I am currently engaged to Kristen Rumfola, and we plan on marrying in the Spring or Fall of next year, hopefully on a beach somewhere in the Carribean.
